Overview

Problem

Access to healthcare is a major hurdle in many countries. People with chronic conditions such as diabetes tend to jam the system especially in places where healthcare has complex access situations.

Solution

Through leveraging new connective capabilities and the increased reliability of IOT, we developed a new product suite to deliver a more seamless way for people to maintain care of their diabetes.

Our Approach

My Contribution

Using systems thinking frameworks, we diagramed the healthcare system and zeroed in on a manageable problem space focused on healthcare issues in Brazil. I conducted a thorough literature review. I learned that access to healthcare could be improved if they could find ways to help patients with chronic conditions self-manage more effectively. Like most places in the world, diabetes is one of the top health issues in Brazil. We then sought to drive our solution towards making it easier for individuals to manage their care in hopes that in doing so would begin to alleviate some of the systemic burdens. We conducted interviews to understand the day-to-day monitoring of diabetes as well as with several experts on healthcare in Brazil. We then developed insights and engaged rapid prototyping which ultimately led to developing a more seamless concept for blood glucose monitoring that allows users to analyze their glucose levels directly from their smartphone.
