mindfield

mindfield is a cyber-physical product service system speculating that if we want to have the ability to use our brain activity (aka our “thoughts” or more specifically: deliberate conscious thought) to control objects then we would likely need to alter the human body in a way that would allow us to network the activity produced through our neurological electrostatic activity. mindfield uses a physics simulator to play with this idea. Using a framework from the medical field, electrodes are placed in specific areas on the skull to catch and relay specific types of brain activity.

echoPath

echoPath demonstrates through simple IOT technology and some not so simple activities, it is possible to construct a rudimentary brain computer interface (BCI) right at home! This project was dreamed up to engage with a challengeing speculative product space, messy data and finding a way to put context around it to make something valuable.
