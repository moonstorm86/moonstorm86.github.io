information

I am an Experience Designer focused on user-centered solutions for consumer and enterprise digital experiences. My expertise is in concept generation, user research, user experience, emerging technology and strategic design. Equipped with a unique set of quantitative, qualitative and systems thinking methods, I can identify subtle nuances and patterns of behavior to generate imaginative, practical solutions.

My experience in research has given my curious nature a structure to investigate important questions with integrity and rigor. My work in education and hospitality has shaped me into an excellent listener who thinks quickly and acts with intent.

I live for the process of discovery and to foster and engage in ongoing dialogue with diverse teams. Whether developing products and experiences, practicing yoga or reconstructing villages in post-disaster Nepal, I drive projects from ideation to implementation to success.
