Overview

Brief

Design a toolbox for storing household tools or a specialist’s toolset in order to gain first-hand understanding of design issues, including:

  • part tolerance
  • unforgiving material constraints
  • part specification
  • material strength
  • functionality (structure + form)

How might we...

Reduce the load of kitchen items to essentials and prioritize protection?

My Role

Product Designer, UX Designer, UX Researcher

Timeline

2 weeks

Deliverables

Secondary Research
Physical Prototype
3D Model
Presentation Poster

Tools

Hardware, Rhino3D, Photoshop / InDesign / Illustrator



Design Outcome

Polyurethane foam-strips along the lid interior creates a friction seal assuring the kit stays shut in transit, while laser cut inserts (not shown) keep a chef’s essential ingredients in place. Embedded magnets on the lid interior keep the kitchen tools in place, like magnetized strips you’d find secured to a kitchen wall. Intentionally designed without a handle, the toolbox can be grasped by hand, cradled under one’s arm or packed easily into a tote bag.
